Private Sharing

For you.

For those you choose.

For no one else.

Encrypted storage

Don't worry if someone gets access to your device: all the information that you add to the app is automatically encrypted.

We take care of it, you just need to choose a strong password.

Encrypted transmission

All the messages that you send through the app are also encrypted.

Even in the case that the communication is somehow intercepted, the eavesdropper would see just gibberish.

Server free

PrivateSharing features peer-to-peer (P2P) connections.

You don't need to rely anymore on a third-party service to share your data. Of course, you can use the app with a server if you choose to do so.
